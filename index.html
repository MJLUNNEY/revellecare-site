The Model

Prescription skincare,
beautifully delivered

When RevelleCare launches in Fall 2026, care will be delivered in three simple steps — from your first consultation to your door.

1

Complete Your Assessment

You will answer a brief intake about your skin, hormonal health, and goals. Our clinical questionnaire will screen for candidacy in minutes.

2

A licensed Canadian healthcare practitioner will review your profile via telemedicine. If clinically appropriate, they will prescribe your personalized formulation.

Your compounded prescription will be dispensed by a licensed Canadian pharmacy and delivered to your door. Ongoing monitoring and renewal will be included.

Understanding the Transition

Why skin changes
during menopause

Estrogen Decline

Estrogen is the primary regulator of skin collagen, hydration, and cell turnover. As levels drop during perimenopause, skin loses up to 30% of its collagen in the first five years.

Barrier Disruption

Reduced estrogen compromises the skin barrier, leading to increased transepidermal water loss, sensitivity, and impaired healing — changes that standard moisturizers cannot fully address.

The Rx Difference

Only prescription-strength topical treatments can act on the dermal receptors responsible for collagen synthesis and elastin production. RevelleCare connects you with a licensed clinician who can prescribe what over-the-counter products simply cannot provide.
