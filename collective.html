Menopause Is a Healthcare Issue.
It's Time We Started Treating It Like One.

For generations, women have been told that menopause is simply something to endure. Hot flashes, sleep disruption, brain fog, mood changes, skin changes — all dismissed as inevitable aging. That explanation overlooks an important truth. Menopause is a significant biological transition driven by changing hormone levels, and it deserves to be treated as the healthcare issue it is.
