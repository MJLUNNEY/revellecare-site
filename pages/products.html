The cornerstone of the RevelleCare protocol. Formulated with two complementary estrogens that act on distinct receptor pathways — estradiol (ERα) drives collagen and elastin production, while estriol (ERβ) repairs the barrier and improves skin flexibility.

Clinical data shows up to +75% increase in epidermal thickness, +38% increase in collagen density, and 61–100% wrinkle reduction with this active combination. Formulated for daily morning use.

  • Restoration of barrier function

Formulation 02

Night
Renewal Cream

Tretinoin 0.05% Cell renewal · Collagen stimulation · MMP inhibition
Estriol 0.03% Barrier support · Retinoid tolerance · ERβ protection

Designed for overnight regeneration. Tretinoin, the gold-standard retinoid, accelerates cellular turnover and inhibits collagen-degrading enzymes — while estriol at a protective low dose maintains barrier integrity and reduces the irritation often associated with retinoid therapy.

The night cycle aligns with your skin's natural circadian repair biology, maximizing tretinoin's mechanism of action. The result: visible improvement in texture, fine lines, and firmness with reduced sensitivity.

Clinical Benefits

  • Accelerated epidermal cell turnover
  • MMP-1 inhibition — reduced collagen breakdown
  • New collagen deposition via RAR + ER dual pathways
  • Reduced retinoid-associated irritation (estriol buffer)
  • Improved fine lines, texture, and pigmentation at 12 weeks
